What This Business Is
Simple Media Masters LLC operates a portfolio of local lead-generation websites that connect homeowners with licensed contractors for home services. The business owns the websites, tracking numbers, and customer relationships. Contractors pay for the leads we send them.
This is a portfolio business, not a single-site business. Long-term value comes from operating 20+ sites across multiple verticals and cities.
The 5-Year Vision

Year 1: 3–6 sites built. $3K–$6K/month revenue by month 12.
Year 2: 8–12 sites. Multiple verticals. $10K–$20K/month.
Year 3: 15–20 sites. First part-time helper. $25K–$40K/month.
Year 5: 30–50 sites. Small team. $50K–$100K/month possible with focused execution.

Targets are aspirational. The metric that matters month-to-month is sites built + sites ranking, not revenue.

AI Philosophy
AI handles: transformation, classification, summarization, drafting, reminding.
AI does not handle: pricing, routing decisions, customer relationships, hiring, money decisions.
The system AI helps with includes:

Cloning sites (templating from one to many)
Drafting follow-up messages to leads (always human-approved before sending)
Classifying calls and leads from transcripts
Generating long-tail SEO content
Monthly reporting and competitive monitoring
Reminding Chris of pending actions

The dashboard and automation system is Phase 4+ work, not pre-launch work. Build it when there's real data to organize.
Legal & Compliance Position
The connector model is legally defensible in California provided:

Site never holds itself out as a contractor
Connector disclosure visible on every page
Internal proof file maintained (CSLB screenshot, insurance, written authorization)
No workmanship guarantees published
No CSLB number displayed (we are not a contractor)

CSLB rules for contractor advertising do not apply to a referral/connector service.
